Last Wednesday, Gilt Groupe hosted the first ever Gilt Expert Talk, featuring presentations from Chris Wanstrath and Leah Culver.

Leah Culver spoke about two open source initiatives that she is a co-author of, <a href="http://www.oauth.net">OAuth</a>, and <a href="http://www.oembed.com">OEmbed</a>.
<a href="http://www.slideshare.net/leahculver/oauth-open-api-authentication">http://www.slideshare.net/leahculver/oauth-open-api-authentication</a>

Chris Wanstrath spoke about Github, and some of the many components that make <a href="http://www.github.com">Github</a> work.
